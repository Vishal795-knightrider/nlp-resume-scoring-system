    PDF Upload

    Upload a PDF resume. pypdf extracts text page-by-page on the server — no disk storage, pure in-memory processing.

    🧹

    Preprocessing

    Text is lowercased, special characters removed, and stopwords filtered — leaving only meaningful domain terms.

    📊

    TF-IDF Vectorization

    Each word gets a weight: high for rare, important words like "Kubernetes"; near-zero for common words like "the".

    📐

    Cosine Similarity

    Measures the angle between the resume vector and JD vector — 0° = perfect match, 90° = no overlap. Length-independent.

    ❌

    Keyword Gap Analysis

    Keywords present in the JD but absent from the resume are flagged as missing — your skill gap at a glance.

    💡

    Smart Suggestions

    Contextual, actionable tips generated from your score, missing keywords, and resume structure analysis.
